Over the past few years, I have repeatedly tried and failed to set up my blog. My motivation to start my personal website stemmed from a perpetual feeling of boredom and a general lack of creativity. Let me give some more background context to this. 

<h2>2008-2012s</h2>
I used to write a lot when I was young. At school, my poems and essays would get published in the school magazine. My short stories were read and appreciated by my English language instructors. I beamed with pride upon their recognition. I used to *create* something back then. My words were my own, an expression of what I genuinely felt and thought. My thoughts were unfiltered and my opinions were strong, undeterred. I never got anyone to seriously review my writing, mostly because I felt scared someone would ask me to change my words which I had carefully and painstakingly selected. 
I recall using the thesaurus functionality in MS Word. I would look up the synonyms of the words I thought rawly, and then I would select the word which would fit best in the sentence.

There has been a marked change in me since I graduated from school. I fell out of habit of writing. I have never delved into its reasons nor I will in the future. It would be a wasteful exercise; time is better spent analysing the changes in me before and after I left writing. I think writing did me a lot of good intellectually. I used to think on my feet. I could frame my points well and I could communicate it across to the other person in a convincing manner.
